Classical Indian Dance forms



Natya Shastra mentions four Pravrittis (traditions, genres) of ancient dance-drama in vogue when it was composed, they are:


  • Avanti (Ujjain, central)
  • Dakshinatya (south)
  • Panchali (north, west)
  • Odra-Magadhi (east)


Sources differ in their list of Indian classical dance forms. The Sangeet Natak Akademi has given recognition to nine Indian dances.
The Indian government's Ministry of Culture includes eleven dance forms. Scholars such as Drid Williams and others include Chhau,
Yaksagana and Bhagavata Mela to the eight classical Indian dances in the Sangeet Natak Akademi list.
